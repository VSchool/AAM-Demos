About This Project

TaskFlow started as a capstone project during my time at V School's Applied AI Mastery program. The idea came from a real pain point: every team I've worked on struggled with prioritizing tasks effectively.

Most project management tools give you a list and leave you to figure out what matters. TaskFlow takes a different approach by using AI to analyze deadlines, dependencies, and team capacity to surface what needs attention right now.

The tech stack includes a React frontend, a Node.js API, and a Python ML service for the prioritization engine. The AI model was fine-tuned on anonymized project data to learn patterns in how successful teams sequence their work.
