The new Rune record drops the night of the show — the label is timing the upload for midnight, mid-set. **The set runs from 10:30 to midnight, then keeps going.**

This is a band show, not a DJ show. Real drums (Theo Vance), bass (Imani Cole), two horns (rotating). The record itself is half-live to tape, and the songs are going to land different in the room than they will in headphones.

**DJ Ahmad** opens with an hour-long set drawing on the record's sample sources — late-70s spiritual jazz, Detroit techno, a couple of South Indian carnatic things you should hear once.

Vinyl pre-orders pick up at the merch table.
