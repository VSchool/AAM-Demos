Static Bloom went quiet last summer when their bassist moved out of state. They're back with a new low end and a handful of songs that didn't make the cut on the last record. The set will be roughly half new material.

**Hush** plays the new record front-to-back. *Soft Constants* came out in February and the band has played exactly two release shows so far; this is the third. Bring the record.

**Lina Park** opens with a 25-minute solo set. Loop pedal, fretless bass, voice.

Pay-what-you-can at the door for anyone under 21 — show ID and ask.
