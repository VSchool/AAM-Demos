Hollow Pines doesn't play the same set twice — the band's been on a slow tour through small rooms for the last six months, and the setlist shifts based on what they played the previous night. Songs from the new record, a couple of old ones, a handful of traditionals.

**All acoustic, all seated.** Two guitars, fiddle, upright bass, three voices. The Loft's piano stays closed for this one.

The merch table is unattended — leave cash in the jar for tapes and t-shirts on the way out.
