Loma and Felt have been trading 8-bar tags since the early 2010s and the back-to-back live mixes have become something of a touring fixture. They share a Discogs hangup, a label, and apparently a couple of unmarked 12-inches that haven't been pressed yet.

This is the only US date on the spring run. **Five hours, no opener, no closer.** Doors at 11, last call at 4. The room holds 180 and these usually sell through advance.

Subsurface is in a converted print shop. Concrete walls, two subs, no lasers. Bring earplugs.

Cash bar. ID required.
