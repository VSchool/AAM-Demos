Maya Reid spent the last twelve months between New York and Tokyo on a quiet, persistent tour. The quartet is back at The Loft for two nights — this is the first.

**Two sets** — 8 PM and 9:45 PM. Table seating, two-drink minimum, no talking through the music. The Loft is a 90-cap room above a bookstore with one of the best pianos in the city.

The current band: Maya Reid (piano), Devon Park (bass), Sam Iyer (drums), guest tenor TBD. The record's standards arrangements have been getting longer in the live setting — expect a 12-minute "Naima."

Tickets are by the table. Two-tops sell first. Reservations through The Loft directly.
